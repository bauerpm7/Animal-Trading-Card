Greyhound

Clocked against other breeds, this canine will nearly always be the victor because they can run as fast as 45 miles per hour.

  • Species: Canus Lupus Familiarus
  • Average Weight: Male 60 - 88 pounds
  • Average Weight: Feale 55 - 75 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years

Greyhounds are sweet, sensitive and reserved. Although they do shed some, they are one breed that does not typically trigger allergies. They can be timid, but if well socialized are affectionate and loyal companions.
